# Diagnosis
## DSM-5 Criteria
- 5+ of the MSIGECAPS symptoms for >= 2 weeks
  - Mood (sadness)
  - Sleep change (insomnia or hypersomnia)
  - Interest (loss of)
  - Guilt/ Worthlessness
  - Energy (decrease)
  - Concentration difficulty
  - Appetite changes
  - Psychomotor slowing
  - Suicide
- One of the symptoms must be mood or loss of interest
